Learn basic rules

Once you learn the basic rules and get used to how everything works together, playing gets much easier.

It is a good plan to learn basic rules or have a friend explain to you

Watch YouTube tutorials

With the little knowledge you have gained learning on your own, you will find useful information on Youtube

Study your mistakes after every match

The key thing is learning from your mmistakes.

The more effort you put into studying some new strategies and then applying them and learning from them
- without repeating the same mistakes in game after game - the faster you're going to learn!
